5AMClub Announces Strategic Bitcoin Reserve & AI Venture Fund

TAIPEI, May 29 2025 – At a global press conference held at 9:30 p.m., 5AMClub announced the creation of a Strategic Bitcoin Reserve and the launch of a dedicated AI Venture Fund. These twin pillars reinforce its mission to cultivate collective growth within its worldwide community, champion decentralization, and accelerate frontier innovation.

The reserve secures long‑term treasury assets in sound digital money, while the fund will seed early‑stage AI projects that share 5AMClub’s “build‑before‑dawn” ethos.
